For occasional use, a mid-range model offers the best balance of cost and performance. Premium models are designed for frequent, demanding use and the extra investment is hard to justify if you only use it a few times a month. Consider how often you will actually use it before deciding on a budget.

Do I need the latest model or is last year's version good enough?

Unless the latest model introduces a significant technology upgrade, last year's version is often the better value. Manufacturers tend to make incremental improvements year over year, and you can usually find the previous generation at a substantial discount while still getting excellent performance.

What is the difference between budget and premium Humidifiers?

Budget models typically use lower-cost materials, have fewer features, and may lack the refinement of premium options. Premium models invest in better engineering, higher-quality components, and more rigorous testing. A well-maintained Humidifier can easily last years beyond a neglected one.
